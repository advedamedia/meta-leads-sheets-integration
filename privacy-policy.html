Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

At Adveda Media, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the Adveda Media Automation Bridge integration application ("App") collects, uses, stores, and shares your data when you connect your Meta (Facebook) and Google accounts to automate lead transfer workflows.

1. Information We Collect and Process

To provide lead synchronization services, our App accesses data from your authorized Meta and Google accounts through official API protocols:

  • Meta Lead Ads Data: When you connect your Facebook page and lead gen forms, we fetch incoming lead data (such as names, emails, phone numbers, and other form submission fields) generated by your target forms.
  • Google Account Permissions: We request access to create, view, edit, and append data to your Google Sheets worksheets so that leads can be automatically recorded. We only read or write to spreadsheets you explicitly select in the App configuration.
  • Tokens and Credentials: We securely store OAuth access and refresh tokens returned by Google and Facebook to keep your automation pipelines running. We do not store or collect your raw login passwords.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To execute real-time synchronization pipelines of Meta Lead Ads form responses directly into your configured Google Sheets.
  • To maintain secure OAuth connections and refresh expired access tokens dynamically in the background.
  • To generate synchronization logs so you can monitor execution history and debug failed events.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third-party marketing networks or external entities.

Your lead data is processed immediately upon receipt from Meta and sent directly to Google APIs to be appended to your selected sheet. We do not store your lead payloads permanently on our servers; they are processed in-memory and transiently logged for sync verification purposes only.

4. Data Retention and Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/SSL) to protect all data transferred between your browser, our servers, Meta Graph APIs, and Google Sheet APIs. Your authorization tokens are stored securely on our hosted backend servers. You can revoke authorization for our App at any time directly through your Google Account Security Settings and Facebook Business/App Settings.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Our App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
